Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task

How to make instant ramen:

  • Gather necessary ingredients and supplies
    • instant ramen packet
    • water
    • pot
    • stove
    • bowl
    • untensil (fork or chopsticks)
    • optional toppings (egg, green onion, hot sauce)
  • open the ramen packet
  • remove the block of noodles and the seasoning packet
  • pour water into the pot
    • check the ramen packet for the recommended amount, usually about 2 cups
  • Place the pot on the stove and turn the burner to high heat
  • Wait for the water to come to a boil
  • Once boiling, carefully add the block of noodles to the pot
  • Let the noodles cook for about 3 minutes
    • Gently stir or break up the noodles as they soften
  • After 2-3 minutes, turn off the stove
  • Add in the contents of the seasoning packet
  • Stir to mix thoroughly
  • Optional: add any extra toppings
  • Carefully pour the ramen into a bowl
  • Let it cool so you don’t burn yourself
  • Enjoy with your preferred utensil!

Task 2: Pseudocode a simple computer game

Rock, Paper, Scissors:

  • Display game instructions
    • Each player chooses either rock, paper, or scissors
    • Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, paper beats rock
  • Accept user input
    • Prompt Player 1 (user) to enter their choice
    • Rock, paper, or scissors
  • Validate user input
    • Check if the user's input is one of the valid options
    • If not, ask again until a valid choice is entered
  • Choose computer move
    • Randomly select rock, paper, or scissors for the computer’s move
  • Compare moves to determine a winner
    • Use rules to check:
      • If both moves are the same: it’s a tie
      • If both moves are different, determine the winner based on the game’s rules
  • Display the result
    • Show both choices and who won the round
  • Ask to play again or end the game
    • Prompt the user if they want to play another round or exit the game
